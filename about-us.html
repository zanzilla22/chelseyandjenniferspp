About Us

6IX Thrifts is a clothing nonprofit organization dedicated to the recycling and reusing of preloved clothing. All profits of the donated clothes sold, along with clothes that remain after a month, will get donated directly to homeless shelters.

At 6IX Thrifts, we are committed to transparency and accountability. We strive to do justice to the clothes entrusted to us by giving them a new life. All of our profits are donated to various organizations that support numerous causes such as homeless shelters, research centers for mental health.
